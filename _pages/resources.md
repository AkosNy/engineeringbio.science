## Plasmids & strains

The pORTMAGE genome-engineering plasmid series and our bacterial strains are
deposited at [Addgene](https://www.addgene.org/search/all/?q=nyerges). Please feel
free to [contact us]({{ '/contact/' | relative_url }}) if you have questions
or would like a fresh stock directly from the lab.

## Protocols

For detailed protocol requests, please get in touch. Course material from the
EMBO Synthetic Biology Course &mdash; built around our pORTMAGE and DIvERGE
genome-engineering technologies &mdash; is available on request.

## Code

Our analysis pipelines and companion software are on
[GitHub](https://github.com/AkosNy).

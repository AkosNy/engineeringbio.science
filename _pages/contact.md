## Joining the lab

We are recruiting a **research assistant** to join our team!

We are actively recruiting **postdoctoral fellows** with strong training in
synthetic biology, genome engineering, directed evolution, or chemical biology,
protein engineering, structural biology, and computational biology. We particularly
welcome applications from candidates pursuing external fellowship support. To apply, please send a description of your scientific interests, your CV, and the names and contact information of three references.

**Undergraduates** with interests &mdash; in molecular biology, biochemistry, computational biology, or a related discipline &mdash; are welcome to inquire.
